1.0
	Initial release

1.1
	Feature: Unpack buffers can be relocated also for sid format.
	Bugfix: Realtime clock was running at wrong speed after invoking cartridge routines.
	Bugfix: Duration of track 00 was sometimes computed incorrectly by birdcruncher.
	Bugfix: Default output name in birdcruncher was a.sid regardless of format.

1.2
	Feature: Possible to mark .sid files as being compatible with both sid models.
	Bugfix: Editor erroneously allowed pulse-width operator '-' (it behaved like '+').
	Bugfix: Birdcruncher didn't handle blank tracks properly.
	Bugfix: Birdcruncher was applying the groove timing backwards.
